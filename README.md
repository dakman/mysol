# MySOL | Blockchain Vault Burner

**MySOL** is a hardware-grade discipline tool that "burns" spending rules directly into the Solana ledger.

## 🚀 The Strategy Presets
The tool includes several presets to help you categorize your funds:

* **Gamba Guard ($50/day - 90 Days):** Limits daily withdrawals to prevent impulsive "all-in" moves. 
* **Fort Knox (0 Limit - 365 Days):** A total HODL vault. 0 can come out until the year is up.
* **Daily Allowance ($20/day - 180 Days):** Perfect for managing a dedicated spending wallet.
* **Gas Saver (0.05 SOL limit - 60 Days):** Ensures you always have gas money left for other trades.
* **The "Slow-Exit" (1.0 SOL/day - 30 Days):** For when you want to take profits but forced to do it slowly over a month.

## 🛠 How It Works
1. **Prepare:** Create a new wallet in Solflare/Phantom.
2. **Burn:** Connect and Sign. The rules are uploaded to a PDA (Program Derived Address).
3. **Self-Destruct:** Once the Enforcement Period ends, the rules **Self-Destruct**. The vault "dissolves" back into a normal wallet with no restrictions.

**CRITICAL:** Rules are immutable until the timer hits zero.
